THE DESIGNER

I am Nathan Laughlin, a midwestern born graphic designer who respects business formalism but also a realist. My strongest in design is branding, I love the idea of upadating creating an image and identity for a larger entity. Alot work is based off hard geometries and simple concepts together making most of my design work minimal and open, staying business casual. Using structure up front and keeping playful in the background.

Im able to work in Adobe programs: Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After Effects, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, and Dreamweaver.
