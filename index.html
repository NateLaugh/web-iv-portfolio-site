THE DESIGNER

I am Nathan Laughlin, a midwestern graphic designer, I have strong respect for business ethics and formalism while maintain a very vibrant and engaging pesonality. My primary design niche is branding, I love the idea of upadating creating an image and identity for a larger entity. Alot work is based off hard geometries and simple concepts, together making most of my design work minimal and open. Playing off of business casual, using structure up front and keeping it playful in the background. #The Mullet Philosophy

Im able to work in Adobe programs: Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After Effects, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, and Dreamweaver.
