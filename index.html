THE DESIGNER

I am Nathan Laughlin, a midwestern born graphic designer and artist established in Lincoln Nebraska. I have strong passion in branding, and have a deep love for history and architecture. Most my work is based off hard geometries and simple concepts together making most of my design work minimal and open, staying business casual. Using structure up front and keeping playful in the background.


My skills as designer are proficient in the following Adobe CC programs: Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After Effects, Lightroom, Premiere Pro, and Dreamweaver. With Photoshop and Illustrator.
