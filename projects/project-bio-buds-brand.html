CLIENT: ICE Entertainment Arts
AUDIENCE PROFILE: Southern California and west coast film and/or entertainment organizations.
APPLICATION: Logo

ICE is an entertainment arts company that targets the southern California film industry such as: LA and Hollywood production business, that look for premium conceptual film designs and effects. Wanting a logo design that would properly show the artistic and cleverness of the brand and bold immersive impression of their business.



CLIENT: Tipsy’s Bar and Lounge
AUDIENCE PROFILE: High income middle class alcohol consumers who want to relax and grab some nice grub with their beer.
APPLICATION: Logo

Tipsy’s is a classy bar and lounge that offers a more relaxing and sophisticated bar atmosphere to its customer. Targeting the upper middle-class consumer between the ages of 35–45 years of age who has additional disposable income and seeks a fun but premium experience when out on the town. This identity attempts to attract this group through a brand that is class and vintage in its design, appearing simple and elegant.



CLIENT: BIO-BUDS
AUDIENCE PROFILE: Audio tech consumers and youth hearing impaired consumer’s ages (16-20)
APPLICATION: Logo

BIO-BUDS is a cybernetic development company in the medical that has developed headphones for not only the everyday audio customer but the hearing impaired youth between the ages of 16-20 who wish to enjoy music as the rest of us do. The identity achieves this through a very calming but minimalistic brand that appears very contemporary and technological.
